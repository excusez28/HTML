LE SSERAFIM

LE SSERAFIM (르세라핌) is a 5-member girl group under HYBE and Source Music. The members currently consist of Kim Chaewon, Sakura, Huh Yunjin, Kazuha, and Hong Eunchae. LE SSERAFIM officially debuted on May 2, 2022 with their 1st mini album “FEARLESS” .On July 19, 2022, it was announced that Kim Garam had left the group
